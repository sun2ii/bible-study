# Genesis - Beginnings

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Author** | Moses (traditional) |
| **Audience** | Israel, preparing to enter the Promised Land |
| **Theme** | Origins - of the world, humanity, sin, and God's redemptive plan |
| **Key Phrase** | "In the beginning..." / "These are the generations of..." |
| **Time Span** | Creation to Joseph's death (~2000+ years) |
| **Key Word** | *Toledot* (generations/account) - appears 11 times |

---

## The Big Picture

Genesis answers: "Where did we come from, and why does God's promise matter?"

### The Six Movements

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 1: ORIGINS (1-3)                                   │
│ Creation → Fall → First Promise                            │
│ "In the beginning... God"                                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 2: JUDGMENT (4-9)                                  │
│ Murder → Corruption → Flood → Covenant                     │
│ "The LORD saw the wickedness... was great"                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 3: SCATTERING (10-11)                              │
│ Nations → Babel → Narrowing to Abram                       │
│ "Come, let us make a name for ourselves"                   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 4: PROMISE (12-25)                                 │
│ Call → Covenant → Test → Fulfillment                       │
│ "I will bless you... all nations blessed through you"      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 5: WRESTLING (26-36)                               │
│ Deception → Conflict → Transformation                      │
│ "Your name shall no longer be Jacob, but Israel"           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ MOVEMENT 6: PROVIDENCE (37-50)                              │
│ Pit → Prison → Palace → Preservation                       │
│ "You meant evil... God meant it for good"                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Key Themes

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Creation & Order** | God brings order from chaos, light from darkness, life from nothing |
| **Fall & Consequences** | Sin enters through disobedience, brings death, curses, separation |
| **Covenant & Promise** | God binds Himself to bless - with Noah, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob |
| **Election & Reversal** | God chooses the younger, the barren, the unlikely |
| **Providence & Sovereignty** | God works through human choices, even evil, for His purposes |
| **Seed & Blessing** | Promise of offspring who will bless all nations |

## The *Toledot* Structure

Genesis is organized by 11 "generations" (*toledot*) markers:

| # | Text | Reference |
|---|------|-----------|
| 1 | "These are the generations of the heavens and earth" | 2:4 |
| 2 | "This is the book of the generations of Adam" | 5:1 |
| 3 | "These are the generations of Noah" | 6:9 |
| 4 | "These are the generations of the sons of Noah" | 10:1 |
| 5 | "These are the generations of Shem" | 11:10 |
| 6 | "These are the generations of Terah" | 11:27 |
| 7 | "These are the generations of Ishmael" | 25:12 |
| 8 | "These are the generations of Isaac" | 25:19 |
| 9 | "These are the generations of Esau" | 36:1 |
| 10 | "These are the generations of Esau" (Edom) | 36:9 |
| 11 | "These are the generations of Jacob" | 37:2 |
