# Matthew 2 - The King's Reception

## Overview
The world responds to the newborn King: Gentiles worship, while Israel's ruler plots murder.

## Chapter Structure

```
Matthew 2
├─ vv. 1-12  : The Magi Visit      (Gentiles seek the King)
├─ vv. 13-15 : Flight to Egypt     (Escape from Herod)
├─ vv. 16-18 : Massacre of Infants (Herod's rage)
└─ vv. 19-23 : Return to Nazareth  (Prophecy fulfilled)
```

---

## vv. 1-12: The Magi Visit the Messiah

### The Contrast

```
TWO RESPONSES TO THE KING

MAGI (Gentile outsiders)          HEROD (Jewish king)
    │                                  │
    ├─ Traveled far                    ├─ Already in Jerusalem
    ├─ Sought to worship               ├─ Sought to kill
    ├─ Followed the star               ├─ Consulted scholars
    ├─ Brought treasures               ├─ Brought deception
    └─ Found Jesus                     └─ Lost everything
```

### The Flow

```
vv. 1-2: MAGI ARRIVE
    │
    "Where is the one born King of the Jews?"
    │
    ├─ Saw His star in the east
    └─ Came to worship
    │
vv. 3-6: HEROD DISTURBED
    │
    ├─ Herod troubled ◄── Threatened by rival king
    │
    ├─ "All Jerusalem with him" ◄── Why? Complicit? Afraid?
    │
    └─ Asks chief priests & scribes
        │
        └─► "Bethlehem" (Micah 5:2)
            │
            "Out of you will come a ruler
             who will shepherd my people Israel"
    │
vv. 7-8: HEROD'S DECEPTION
    │
    ├─ Calls Magi secretly
    ├─ Asks exact time of star
    └─ "Report back so I can worship too" ◄── LIE
    │
vv. 9-12: MAGI FIND JESUS
    │
    ├─ Star leads them to the house
    │
    ├─ "Overjoyed" ◄── Not just happy, MEGA joy
    │
    ├─ Saw the child with Mary
    │
    ├─ Worshiped Him
    │
    ├─ Opened treasures:
    │   ├─ Gold        ◄── King
    │   ├─ Frankincense ◄── Priest (used in temple worship)
    │   └─ Myrrh       ◄── Death (burial spice)
    │
    └─ Warned in dream → Return by another route
```

### The Gifts

| Gift | Symbolic Meaning | Jesus Is... |
|------|------------------|-------------|
| **Gold** | Royalty, wealth | King |
| **Frankincense** | Priestly incense, prayer | Priest / God |
| **Myrrh** | Embalming spice | Mortal / Sacrifice |

### Key Observations

| Detail | Significance |
|--------|--------------|
| **"Magi from the east"** | Likely Persia/Babylon - Gentiles! |
| **"King of the Jews"** | Political title - threatens Herod |
| **Star** | Supernatural sign, possibly angelic |
| **"House" not manger** | Time has passed since Luke 2 birth |
| **Herod asks "exact time"** | Calculating age for later massacre |
| **Dream warning** | God protects His Son |

### The Irony

```
WHO KNEW WHERE MESSIAH WOULD BE BORN?
    │
    ├─ Chief priests → Knew Scripture (Micah 5:2)
    ├─ Teachers of law → Knew prophecy
    └─ Yet... NONE of them went to Bethlehem!

GENTILE MAGI
    │
    ├─ Didn't have the Scriptures
    ├─ Traveled 1000+ miles
    └─ Actually FOUND and WORSHIPED Him
```

### One-Line Cut
**The religious insiders knew the address but never visited - the pagan outsiders traveled a thousand miles to worship.**

## vv. 13-18: Flight to Egypt & Massacre

### The Flow

```
vv. 13-15: ESCAPE TO EGYPT
    │
    Angel appears to Joseph (2nd dream)
    │
    ├─ "Get up"
    ├─ "Take the child and his mother"
    ├─ "Escape to Egypt"
    └─ "Herod is going to search for the child to kill him"
    │
    Joseph obeys IMMEDIATELY
    │
    ├─ Got up
    ├─ Left during the night ◄── No delay
    └─ Stayed until Herod's death
    │
    PROPHECY FULFILLED: Hosea 11:1
    │
    └─► "Out of Egypt I called my son"

vv. 16-18: MASSACRE OF THE INNOCENTS
    │
    Herod realizes he was outwitted
    │
    ├─ Furious ◄── Rage of a threatened tyrant
    │
    └─► Orders: Kill ALL boys
        ├─ Location: Bethlehem + vicinity
        ├─ Age: 2 years and under
        └─ Based on: Time learned from Magi
    │
    PROPHECY FULFILLED: Jeremiah 31:15
    │
    └─► "A voice is heard in Ramah,
         weeping and great mourning,
         Rachel weeping for her children
         and refusing to be comforted,
         because they are no more."
```

### Joseph's Pattern

| Dream # | Message | Response |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **1st** (1:20) | Take Mary as wife | Obeyed |
| **2nd** (2:13) | Flee to Egypt | Obeyed immediately, at night |
| **3rd** (2:19) | Return to Israel | *[coming next]* |
| **4th** (2:22) | Go to Galilee | *[coming next]* |

**Joseph = Man of silent obedience. Never speaks in Scripture, only acts.**

### The Prophecies

| Prophecy | Original Context | Matthew's Application |
|----------|------------------|----------------------|
| **Hosea 11:1** | Israel (nation) called out of Egypt in Exodus | Jesus relives Israel's story - the true Son |
| **Jeremiah 31:15** | Rachel weeping for exiles to Babylon | Innocent children killed for the Messiah's sake |

### Jesus Relives Israel's Story

```
ISRAEL                          JESUS
    │                               │
    ├─ Called God's "son"           ├─ The true Son of God
    │  (Exodus 4:22)                │
    │                               │
    ├─ Went down to Egypt           ├─ Fled to Egypt
    │  (famine, Joseph)             │  (Herod's threat)
    │                               │
    ├─ Called out of Egypt          ├─ Called out of Egypt
    │  (Exodus)                     │  (after Herod dies)
    │                               │
    ├─ Passed through water         ├─ Baptism (ch. 3)
    │  (Red Sea)                    │
    │                               │
    ├─ Tested 40 years in wilderness├─ Tested 40 days (ch. 4)
    │  (failed)                     │  (succeeds)
    │                               │
    └─ Received Law on mountain     └─ Gives Law on mountain (ch. 5-7)
        (Sinai)                         (Sermon on the Mount)

JESUS = The True Israel who succeeds where Israel failed
```

### The Horror of Herod

| Detail | Significance |
|--------|--------------|
| **"2 years and under"** | Jesus may have been ~2 years old by now |
| **Bethlehem's size** | Small village - maybe 20-30 boys killed |
| **Historical Herod** | Killed his own sons, paranoid tyrant |
| **"Rachel weeping"** | Rachel's tomb was near Bethlehem |

### One-Line Cut
**Jesus escapes to Egypt so He can be called OUT of Egypt - reliving Israel's exodus as the true Son.**

## vv. 19-23: Return to Nazareth

### The Flow

```
vv. 19-21: ANGEL'S 3RD MESSAGE
    │
    Herod dies
    │
    Angel appears to Joseph (3rd dream)
    │
    ├─ "Get up"
    ├─ "Take the child and his mother"
    ├─ "Go to the land of Israel"
    └─ "Those who were trying to take the child's life are dead"
    │
    Joseph obeys
    │
    └─► Heads toward Israel

vv. 22-23: REDIRECT TO GALILEE
    │
    Problem: Archelaus reigning in Judea
    │
    ├─ Son of Herod ◄── Just as brutal as his father
    ├─ Joseph afraid
    │
    Angel warns in dream (4th dream)
    │
    └─► Withdraws to Galilee
        │
        └─► Settles in Nazareth
    │
    PROPHECY FULFILLED:
    │
    └─► "He would be called a Nazarene"
```

### Joseph's Four Dreams (Complete)

| Dream # | Location | Message | Response |
|---------|----------|---------|----------|
| **1st** (1:20) | Nazareth | Take Mary as wife | Obeyed |
| **2nd** (2:13) | Bethlehem | Flee to Egypt | Obeyed immediately |
| **3rd** (2:19) | Egypt | Return to Israel | Obeyed |
| **4th** (2:22) | Judea | Go to Galilee | Obeyed |

**Pattern: God guides step by step. Joseph never sees the full plan - just the next move.**

### The "Nazarene" Prophecy

```
Problem: No OT verse says "He will be called a Nazarene"
    │
    Matthew says "prophets" (plural) - not one specific verse
    │
    Possible meanings:
    │
    ├─ נֵצֶר (Netzer) = "Branch" (Isaiah 11:1)
    │   │
    │   └─► "A Branch will come from Jesse's roots"
    │        Nazareth sounds like Netzer
    │
    ├─ Nazareth = Despised town
    │   │
    │   └─► "Can anything good come from Nazareth?" (John 1:46)
    │        Messiah would be despised (Isaiah 53)
    │
    └─ נָזִיר (Nazir) = Set apart / consecrated
        │
        └─► Jesus set apart from birth
```

### Why Galilee Matters

| Region | Reputation | Significance |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Judea** | Religious center, Jerusalem | Where you'd expect Messiah |
| **Galilee** | "Galilee of the Gentiles" | Backwater, mixed population |
| **Nazareth** | Tiny, insignificant | No prophecy, no prestige |

**God hides the King in the last place anyone would look.**

### One-Line Cut
**The King of the Jews grows up in a town so insignificant it becomes an insult - "Nazarene."**
