## Old Testament

### Law (Torah/Pentateuch)
| Book | Theme | Key Idea |
|------|-------|----------|
| Genesis | Beginnings | Creation, Fall, Abraham, Jacob, Joseph |
| Exodus | Redemption | Deliverance from Egypt, Law given |
| Leviticus | Holiness | "Be holy, for I am holy" |
| Numbers | Wandering | 40 years in wilderness |
| Deuteronomy | Covenant Renewal | Moses' final sermons |

### History
| Book | Theme |
|------|-------|
| Joshua | Conquest of Canaan |
| Judges | Cycles of rebellion |
| Ruth | Loyalty & redemption |
| 1-2 Samuel | Rise of kingship (Saul, David) |
| 1-2 Kings | Divided kingdom to exile |
| 1-2 Chronicles | Judah's history retold |
| Ezra | Return & rebuild temple |
| Nehemiah | Rebuild walls |
| Esther | Providence in exile |

### Poetry/Wisdom

|------|-------|

| Ecclesiastes | Meaning of life |
| Song of Solomon | Love & intimacy |
